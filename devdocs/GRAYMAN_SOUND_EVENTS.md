## Sound Design Notes
- Purple/lavender psychic aesthetic throughout
- Reality-warping, spacetime manipulation themes
- Psychic/psionic energy (not physical)
- Electromagnetic/dimensional sounds for teleportation
- Mind-force and mental energy for attacks
- Unstable psychic energy for echo explosion
